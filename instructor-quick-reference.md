# BUILD 1: YOUR SECOND BRAIN — Quick Reference (Print This)

**45 min | Demo (Arjun's data) → CLAUDE.md (theirs) → Second Brain (theirs)**

---

## PART 1 — THE WOW (8 min) *You demo, they watch*

**Prompt 1:** "Look at messy-workspace. Group by theme, flag what's important, tell me what this person's life looks like."
- CALLOUT: "It READ every file, not just listed them."

**Prompt 2:** "Organize messy-workspace into folders. Rename bad names. Create a README."
- CALLOUT: "See Claude asking permission? That's the permission model. You approve everything."

**Prompt 3:** "What did Acme Corp say about the timeline? What's the Q1 budget situation?"
- CALLOUT: "I didn't say which file. It searched everything."

**TRANSITION:** "That was Arjun's mess. Now you'll teach Claude who YOU are."

---

## PART 2 — CLAUDE.md + BEFORE/AFTER (15 min) *The key moment*

**BEFORE (1 min):** Everyone asks: "Write a one-paragraph executive summary of Arjun's messy-workspace files."
- "Notice: generic. Everyone got the same answer."

**INTERVIEW (8 min):** Prompt on screen:
> "Create my personal CLAUDE.md. Interview me about: 1) role/industry 2) current priorities 3) how I like info presented 4) what I use Claude for. Ask 2-3 questions at a time."

- TAs: walk room. Help stuck people: "What did you do at work last Monday?"
- If too many questions: "Say 'that's enough, create it now.'"

**AFTER (2 min):** "Ask the same question about Arjun's files, but frame it for ME based on CLAUDE.md."

> **SAY THIS:** "Same files. Same question. Completely different answer. The AI didn't get smarter — you gave it your brain."

**ITERATE (2 min):** "Read my CLAUDE.md, suggest 3 things to add."
- "Build, test, iterate. That loop is everything."

---

## PART 3 — BUILD YOUR SECOND BRAIN (12 min) *Pivot to their data*

> **SAY THIS:** "Arjun was the demo. Now we leave him behind. From here, it's YOUR life."

**CREATE STRUCTURE (7 min):** Prompt on screen:
> "Set up a 'my-second-brain' folder. Based on CLAUDE.md, create folders for: projects, meetings, templates, reference, daily log. Add READMEs and 2-3 templates for my role. Update CLAUDE.md with the folder map."

- CALLOUT: "A PM got different folders than a founder. Claude used YOUR CLAUDE.md."

**SEED IT (3 min):** Three options:
- A: Paste real notes from phone/email
- B: Brain-dump 3-5 things on your plate
- C: "Generate realistic sample files for my role"

**TEST (2 min):** "What should I focus on this week? Where are the relevant files?"

---

## PART 4 — MAKE IT STICK (10 min)

**CLAUDE.md EXPLAINER (2 min):**
```
~/.claude/CLAUDE.md      → loads EVERY session (your identity)
~/my-second-brain/CLAUDE.md → loads in that folder (project context)
```
- "Set it up once. Claude knows you forever. Student handbook has the steps."
- Hidden folders: Mac `Cmd+Shift+.` / Windows View > Hidden items

**DAILY WORKFLOW (3 min):** "Add a 'Daily Workflow' section to CLAUDE.md — morning review, capture during day, weekly review."

**MONDAY TEST (4 min):** "Ask a real Monday morning question for your role."

**LAND IT (1 min):** "This CLAUDE.md follows you all day. By Build 5, Claude knows who you are, writes in your voice, and ships products for you. It all starts here."
