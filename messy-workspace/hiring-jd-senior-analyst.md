# Job Description - Senior Business Analyst (DRAFT)

**Title:** Senior Business Analyst
**Team:** Product
**Reports to:** Me
**Location:** Bangalore (Hybrid - 3 days in office)

## Why this role
We're growing fast and making decisions based on gut feel more than we should. Need someone who can turn our data into insights that drive product and business decisions.

## What you'll do
- Own the analytics stack (Amplitude, Metabase, internal dashboards)
- Build and maintain product metrics dashboards (daily active users, feature adoption, funnel conversion)
- Run deep-dive analyses for product decisions (pricing, feature prioritization, market sizing)
- Create the quarterly business review deck with Vikram
- Support customer health scoring model (currently manual, needs automation)
- Partner with Meera on user research data synthesis

## You should have
- 4-6 years in analytics/BI role (product company preferred)
- Strong SQL. Can write complex queries without hand-holding.
- Experience with at least one BI tool (Amplitude, Mixpanel, Looker, Metabase)
- Ability to communicate insights to non-technical stakeholders
- Bonus: Python/R for statistical analysis
- Bonus: Experience with A/B testing frameworks

## Compensation
- INR 18-24L CTC depending on experience
- ESOPs (standard for the level)
- Standard benefits (health insurance, learning budget, etc.)

## Interview Process
1. Screening call with me (30 min)
2. Take-home case study (analyze a dataset, present findings)
3. Technical round with Suresh (SQL + data modeling)
4. Culture fit with Priya and Meera
