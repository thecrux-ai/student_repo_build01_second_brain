# Competitor Analysis - DRAFT (started Jan 10, not finished)

## CompetitorX
- Raised Series C ($45M) in November
- Launched self-serve tier at $49/mo (we don't have self-serve yet)
- Mobile app is better than ours (4.5 stars vs our 3.2)
- Pricing: 30% cheaper on enterprise tier
- Weakness: No API marketplace, limited integrations, poor India support

## CompetitorY
- Smaller but growing fast in India specifically
- Their sales team is aggressive - poaching our leads in Mumbai and Delhi
- Product is simpler but "good enough" for SMBs
- Pricing: Freemium model pulling in volume
- Weakness: Doesn't scale well for enterprise, poor analytics

## CompetitorZ
- Enterprise-only, not really competing on SMB
- But some of our mid-market customers are evaluating them
- Strong on security/compliance features (SOC2, ISO certified)
- Weakness: Expensive, slow to implement, terrible UX
