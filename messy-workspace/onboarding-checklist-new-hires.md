# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

For the 2 new devs joining Suresh's team (Jan 6)

## Before Day 1
- [ ] Laptop ordered and configured
- [ ] Accounts created: Slack, Jira, GitHub, Notion, Figma (viewer)
- [ ] Buddy assigned (ask Suresh for recommendations)
- [ ] Welcome email sent with first-week schedule
- [ ] Add to relevant Slack channels: #engineering, #product-updates, #random

## Week 1
- [ ] Welcome meeting with me (30 min - company context, product vision)
- [ ] Deep dive with Suresh (architecture, codebase, dev practices)
- [ ] Shadow a customer call with CSM team
- [ ] Read: Product strategy doc, Q4 retro, competitor analysis
- [ ] First PR: Fix a small bug from the backlog (Suresh to pick)

## Week 2
- [ ] 1:1 with me: How's it going? Any confusion?
- [ ] Join sprint planning
- [ ] Pair programming session with a senior dev
- [ ] Present "What I've learned so far" at Friday team sync (5 min)

## Week 3-4
- [ ] Own a small feature end-to-end
- [ ] 30-day check-in with me and Suresh

## Notes
- Suresh's team is working on Project Alpha infra. New hires should ramp on that codebase specifically.
- One of the hires has ML background - could be useful for the chatbot idea later
